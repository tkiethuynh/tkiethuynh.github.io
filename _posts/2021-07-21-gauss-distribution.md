## Guassian distribution
Guass distribution or normal distribution is defined by:
$$
\mathcal N(x|\mu,\sigma^2)=\frac{1}{(2\pi\sigma^2)^{1/2}}\exp\left[\frac{1}{2\sigma^2}(x-\mu)^2\right]
$$w
